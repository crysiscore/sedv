Description

Customers scan & pay QR codes, payments to merchants such as Shopping & Retail, Accounts & Bills, TV Subscription, Health & Beauty, VodaShop, Utilities and more.
Merchants with M-Pesa account receive payments from any customer with the App. No integration required. Generate QR codes, pin your location on the App so customers can find you.
